Pick and Place for Baxter

Ein is a program for pick-and-place for Baxter. If you want to pick up objects, you can quickly and automatically use Baxter's wrist camera to create a model for detecting, localizing, and manipulating tabletop objects. Our stack is capable of automatically mapping objects autonomously for long periods of time, and we encourage you to participate in this task as part of the Million Object Challenge.
